Name: Bitcoin >Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, created in 2009 by an unknown person using the alias Satoshi Nakamoto. It operates without a central authority and is powered by blockchain technology.
Name: Ethereum Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain platform that establishes a peer-to-peer network that securely executes and verifies application code, called smart contracts. Smart contracts allow participants to transact with each other without a trusted central authority.
Name: Binance Binance is an online exchange where users can trade cryptocurrencies. It supports hundreds of the most commonly traded cryptocurrencies. Binance provides a crypto wallet for traders to store their electronic funds. The exchange has supporting services for users to earn interest or transact using cryptocurrencies.
